- `Bio:` Gary Marcus is a cognitive scientist and artificial intelligence researcher. He is the founder and CEO of Robust.AI, a company that is developing artificial intelligence systems that can learn and adapt to new environments. Marcus is also a professor of psychology and neuroscience at New York University and the author of several books on artificial intelligence, including "The Algebraic Mind" and "Rebooting AI". His research focuses on understanding the foundations of intelligence and developing artificial intelligence systems that can learn and adapt to new tasks. Marcus is a leading researcher in the field of artificial intelligence and has made significant contributions to our understanding of the nature of intelligence and how it can be artificially replicated.
