Bio:
Kai-Fu Lee is a Chinese-American computer scientist and artificial intelligence expert. He is the founder and CEO of Sinovation Ventures, a venture capital firm that invests in technology companies, and is the author of the book "AI Superpowers: China, Silicon Valley, and the New World Order." Lee has also served as the president of Google China and as a vice president at Microsoft. He is known for his work in the fields of artificial intelligence and machine learning, and has received numerous awards and honors for his contributions to the field.
