Viktor Pelevin is a Russian novelist and playwright. He is known for his science fiction and fantasy novels, which often include elements of satire and philosophical commentary. Pelevin's works have been translated into numerous languages and have gained a large following in Russia and around the world. Some of his most famous works include the novels "Omon Ra," "The Life of Insects," and "The Yellow Arrow." Pelevin has won several awards for his writing, and he is considered one of the most important and influential contemporary Russian writers.
